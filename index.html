Runtastic Steps

is an advanced step counter app with training plans.

_goal To design an app that works for less sporty people and helps them to enter the world of Runtastic apps.
_workflow To generate a rough idea we started with a short design sprint. I was the only product designer in an interdisciplinary scrum team. While working on the mvp and adding more and more features to it, I did several smaller and bigger user tests. I prototyped ui animation mostly with Principle.

tuifly.com

is an airline which also sells flights from foreign providers.

_goal To redesign the website and the search flow, optimised for user guidance and conversion.
_workflow We started with a focus group and a competitive analysis. Based on the results I worked on the conception and the interaction design. In collaboration with a ui designer we built a high fidelity prototype and tested it with users.

Volkswagen

is a well-known car manufacturer.

_goal Building a car configurator for the web that makes the process of configuring a car understandable and easy to use.
_workflow This was a huge project. We created a living styleguide to align within the design team and to collaborate with the developers. We worked in several scrum teams to cover the different aspects in parallel. For an external user test we built an extremely high fidelity prototype in html/css.

paydirekt

is a payment provider integrated into most german bank accounts.

_goal To design a smooth and seemless payment experience.
_workflow The basic service design was already done when I joined the project. I sketched a user flow and wireframes to discuss this with the different stakeholders. Based on the feedback me and a ui designer created the look and feel.
