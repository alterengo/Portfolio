Boost engagement with challenges

Runtastic/Adidas ‚Ä¢ 2018-2019

How can we boost the users' engagement and selling branded challenges. How can we help users to surpass themselves by competing with others.


‚Üì Show more


role I was the responsible designer for social engagement in the Runtastic App. This project not only augmented the app's social aspects but also aligned closely with business objectives, demonstrating the impact of my role from conception to launch.

challenge How to create users engagement? Engagement comes from users wanting to accept a real challenge with social interactivity. And a real challenge needs to match the individual balanced level of difficulty and feasibility.

workflow It was simply not possibel to ask users what they needed to be properly engaged by a challenge. So we needed to implement fast and learn from the feedback of active users. We started with a deep competitor analysis and a short design sprint to get a rough idea what we wanted. As soon as we had the basics we started with rounds of surveys and user interviews to get an idea of what we needed to do to increase the desire for deeper participation in our challenges.

Transforming seperate applications into a unified experience

Business Keeper (now EQS) ‚Ä¢ 2020-2021

I led a transformative redesign of their b2b compliance tool suite to address customer concerns about usability and appearance.


‚Üì Show more


As the lead designer at Business Keeper (now EQS) hired in 2019, I led a transformative redesign of their b2b compliance tool suite to address customer concerns about usability and appearance. I conducted a thorough analysis of all applications, identifying key interaction and navigation patterns. From there, I created a unified design system, defining basic components, a grid system, and consistent navigation behaviors - the base for a complete redesign and alignment of all applications. To integrate the experience even more and create a seamless application transition we worked on a centralized dashboard application. Despite technical differences across products, I ensured the gradual implementation of this design system - collaborating closely with the development teams. My role extended to mentoring a junior designer, collectively enhancing internal tools and regularly updating our design approach.

As a passionate photographer I love my camera but I always wonder why the digital experience is so bad. And that seems to be valid for basically all cameras on the market.
